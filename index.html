Welcome to TalTech HPC Centre

TalTech HPC Centre provides high-performance computing (HPC) resources and support for research and academic purposes at Tallinn University of Technology (TalTech).

LUMI Supercomputer

LUMI is one of the most powerful supercomputers in the world, located in Finland.
It is part of the EuroHPC Joint Undertaking and provides cutting-edge computational capabilities for researchers across Europe.
