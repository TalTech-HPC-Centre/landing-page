TalTech HPC Centre

TalTech HPC Centre provides high-performance computing resources and support for research and academic purposes.

LUMI Supercomputer

LUMI is one of the most powerful supercomputers in the world, located in Finland.

LUMI offers GPUs, CPUs and storage capacity for researchers affiliated with an Estonian higher-education or research institute as well as for commercial users from an organization established or located in Estonia.
